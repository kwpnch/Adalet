FIGHT FOR JUSTICE

The true story of one person's ten-year struggle for rights against corruption, crime, and institutional collapse in an apartment building in Ankara.

SUMMARY

Struggle in the Apartment

For over a decade, they made apartment life unbearable. Even the summary is as long as below, and this is just the beginning:

  • They used the front garden with an illegal structure: They filled the 100m2 reinforced concrete illegal structure under the front garden, which is the common area of the apartment, with printing machines and used it for 30 years. In fact, this illegal structure existed during the first licensing 60 years ago. So, the Municipality has been involved from the very beginning.
  • They placed an acquaintance with a criminal record in the janitor's apartment: To defraud the SGK (Social Security Institution), they showed the janitor as a tenant and employed him without insurance. The man didn't do any work properly anyway, and then he quit completely. I cleaned the apartment from our floor down many times.
  • They drilled a hole in the terrace and directed rainwater into the sewage pipe: For 8 years, my apartment on the first floor was flooded during every heavy rain.
  • They had the reinforced concrete apartment registered as Masonry in the title deed: There are columns and beams in the building. They have been gaining advantage for 60 years.
  • There are allegations of sexual harassment, molestation, and prostitution: The individual living in the janitor's apartment for free did not stay still; there are incidents of harassment and molestation against young girls and even trans individuals. The police raided the janitor's apartment many times. Plainclothes police officers investigated the janitor's apartment asking "has anyone seen foreign national women". I used to wait for my sister at the windows when she was coming.
  • They let the janitor's apartment use electricity illegally: Enerjisa sealed the apartment's electricity meter many times, leaving the apartment without electricity.
  • Moreover, the management and their instigators supported all these crimes and criminals: They openly confessed everything and said "so what", "what part of us is right anyway".
  • They intimidated everyone with insults and threats, I was left alone: They tried to silence everyone who objected to these unlawfulnesses, especially me, by shouting, insulting, and threatening. I was the only one left struggling against these in the apartment; everyone else fell silent. Everyone who passed for a "decent" person and claimed to support justice disappeared, I was left alone.
  • Finally, they tried to silence me by breaking my jaw: Because I did not bow to insults and threats and filed a complaint, they finally tried to silence me by assaulting me and breaking my jaw. With verbal violence, they hospitalized my 91-year-old mother; she had surgery.
  • They decided to demolish the apartment to destroy evidence: While my treatment was ongoing, my jaw fixed and unable to eat or speak, together with all those "decent" people, they held a meeting where they openly confessed to the illegal structure and by majority vote decided to demolish the apartment that had been renovated just 2 years ago. Police chiefs, lawyers, mediators, teachers, that is, all those "decent" people from every profession united with these crime machines.
  • They are blinded by money: I was the only homeowner living full-time in the apartment anyway, the others were just eating tenant money. Therefore, they didn't care that the apartment was in filth, flooded with sewage water, or that the man in the janitor's apartment was harassing young girls (if they raped someone, they would say "so what" and turn their backs and leave). Now, when the illegal structure, SGK, illegal drainage, title deed irregularity, and other penalty possibilities emerged, that is, when the subject became money, they changed sides without any shame. "Justice" was immediately thrown into the trash. They gave up morality for money.
  • We had to move to another place because the threats continued: The manager who assaulted me was waiting at the entrance of the apartment with a stick, so I couldn't go to my treatment for 2 weeks. Because they walked over me when they saw me, I could only go grocery shopping after 8 pm. The manager was disturbing me by hitting the ceiling of the shop (the floor of my apartment) with a stick. Since the threats and harassment did not end, we left our apartment and the city in the middle of the night, leaving my treatment unfinished.

In this country, when it comes to money and personal interest, the first things to go into the trash are always justice and morality. They try to console themselves by saying "Well, the world is like this, what can we do". People who defend justice and morality and are truly decent are labeled as "abnormal, asocial, anti-social", "characterless" and "troublemaker" (In the first hearing of the assault case, the defendant's lawyer started to explain that my character is bad by analyzing my character, fortunately the judge stopped him saying "let's not get into those subjects"). This is how they brought not only this apartment but this entire country to this state.

However, the rule is this: Before you start talking about the country's problems, you will clean your own backyard. While the sons of the homeland fall martyr to protect the borders, if you sit on your ass and do not intervene even in what is happening right next to you on the homeland soil, if you close your eyes and plug your ears, the homeland is already lost. Who is the real enemy and where? Look right next to you first. Look in the mirror first!

Struggle in Exile

The above was just the beginning. Now a legal struggle that would last for years had begun.

  • Insult case: In fact, about 8 months after I complained about this act which had the purpose of blackmail with the insult "Pervert", an insult lawsuit was filed. Although it was completely in my favor, I objected to the decision. The court accepted it and the case file was given to another court. The new court, simplifying the event even further, accepted it as a crime qualification that can be closed with prepayment and is about to drop the case. Besides, the Prosecutor's Office had given a Decision of Non-Prosecution (KYOK) to my illegal structure report in the same complaint.
  • Assault case: The defendants used the man in the janitor's apartment as a false witness twice and tried to show me as the aggressor. The first one was rejected by the Criminal Judgeship of Peace on Duty. In the second one, the judge looking at the assault case listened to the false witness. But the court still sees this assault act where they broke my jaw as an isolated ordinary assault; therefore, a lawsuit continues as if it has nothing to do with the events above.
